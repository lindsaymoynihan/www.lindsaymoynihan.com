Original Paintings

On the Wall

I love to paint BIG! It's so fun and freeing to move my brushes around with big, bold expressive movements. I also enjoy whimsy, anyone who knows me will tell you I love all things magical. Whenever I have the opportunity to paint a whimsical wall mural, I jump at the chance! Here are a few of my most memorable mural projects.

When I was approached by Julia, the owner of Fly Fit in Kalamazoo, MI and asked to paint a wing mural on the studio wall, I jumped at the opportunity. I love the community of strong, confident women who not only take classes at the studio but also support, encourage and empower each other. I love knowing that countless women have posed in front of these wings to mark special occasions as well as fitness accomplishments at the studio.
