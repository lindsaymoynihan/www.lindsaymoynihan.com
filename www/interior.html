Interior Spaces

I LOVE interior design! I'll say it again...I LOVE interior design! Am I a total amateur? Yes! Am I also a total geek about it? Also yes! I'm the person who instagram stalks all of the most colorful and funky designers. I subscribe to design blogs, magazines and lets not even get started on the gazillion pinterest boards I've created. I realize none of this makes me a professional interior designer by far. I'm completely self taught but I'm definitely starting to develop my own style when it comes to interior spaces. I'm a maximalist who loves color. You could describe my style as boho, eclectic, bright, funky, possibly a bit over the top? You decide!
